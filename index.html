My name is Leslie Zurita-Zavala and I'm in my third year of college, majoring in interactive and graphic design. I instantly fell in love with design ever since I had a project back in high school which was to create a book cover. I am currently working as an intern for the annuel Made in Millersville conference and have created some logos along with posters for the event.

When it comes to aesthetics, I'm both drawn to art deco and pop art. My ultimate goal is to help small businesses by providing them with a unique brand that will assist them in their journey and ensure that they stand out from the rest.
